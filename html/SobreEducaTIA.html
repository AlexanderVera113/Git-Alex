Sobre Nosotros

Empoderando educadoras y al servicio de tu vocación.

EducaTIA se dedica a proporcionar a las educadoras de parvulo herramientas y recursos para crear experiencias de aprendizaje inclusivas y atractivas para niños con Necesidades Educativas Especiales (NEE).

Our Mission

To revolutionize early childhood education by providing educators with innovative, accessible, and effective resources that cater to the diverse needs of all children, fostering a supportive community for collaboration and growth.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child, regardless of their abilities, has the opportunity to thrive in a nurturing and stimulating educational environment, supported by empowered and confident educators.

Our Core Values

Inclusivity

Every child deserves to thrive. We champion diversity and create opportunities for all.

Collaboration

We foster a community where educators connect, share, and learn from one another.

Innovation

We are committed to continuous improvement, seeking new and better ways to serve.
